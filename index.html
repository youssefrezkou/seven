WHO ARE WE

-    With long experience of its founder and that of his incorporated staff in diversified fields related to maritime profession and land transport , 7 SEAS EXPERT & ADVISORY newly created puts its skills and expertise at your disposal.
-    Our staff are highly qualified marine surveyors and expert maritime witness. Our principal marine surveyors and other staff are former master mariner, chief engineer and professional experts in the field.
-    7 SEAS EXPERT & ADVISORY is committed to providing their clients with advice and preserving their interests in the event of damage affecting faculties and insured equipment.
-    We are pleased to represent and work with shippers, cargo owners, receivers, shipowners, charters, P&I clubs and other clients.
-    We have a highly professional and specifically trained group of surveyors for Draft Survey, Pre-Loading Survey, Discharge Survey, Cargo Damage Survey, Container Repair Inspection, Container Cleanliness & Suitability, Technical condition Inspection, Bunker Survey, Container Damage Survey, Hull & Machinery Damage Survey, Break Bulk Cargo/ Heavy lift, Flat rack Securing and Inspection, Discharge Survey, Pre-loading Survey, Draft Survey.
-    We are serving at all the ports in Morocco including Tangier, Casablanca, Jorf Lasfar, Safi , Agadir as well as other ports.
-    7 SEAS Surveying teams are based in Tangier, Casablanca and Agadir.
-    As a company with national connections, 7 SEAS EXPERT & ADVISORY is proudly partnered with some of the best Marine Surveyors in the business and can meet your every Marine Surveying need in other Moroccan ports.
