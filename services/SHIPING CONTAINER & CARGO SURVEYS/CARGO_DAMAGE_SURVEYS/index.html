Container Damage Surveys


When things go wrong with Cargo and Shipping, they can go very wrong, very fast. If your container and/or goods were or might have been damaged in transport, call BMP Marine to have us perform a Cargo Damage Survey right away!

We will survey the Cargo with the utmost care are accurately report any damaged caused by fire, explosions, water, other liquids, shortages, or other Cargo related damages.

Container and Cargo Damage Surveys are required by many insurance companies in order to file an accurate, third party verified loss claim.
